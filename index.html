⚡ Transparency: energy used to build this page

This page was developed through a conversation with Claude (a large language model), covering design, build, and adding resources. Based on published estimates of roughly 0.34–1 Wh per query for frontier-scale models, the total energy used to build it is estimated at about 5–20 Wh — comparable to running an LED bulb for 30 minutes to 2 hours. Once live, the page itself is a static site and uses a negligible amount of energy to load. We share this in the spirit of the transparency we advocate for: AI tools have a real if modest per-session footprint, and the goal is more intentional choices, not avoiding AI entirely. Figures are estimates and vary by model, hardware, and query length.
